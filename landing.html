Argiconnect

Agriconnect is a user-friendly platform that generates opportunities for farmers to connect with industry buyers and with fellow farmers to form a cohesive community.

The Problem.......

  • One of the main problems that exists today is the lack of a proper marketplace for farmers, this restricts them from getting connected with big industrial buyers.
  • They are forced to sell their produce for a lesser price and have to endure losses which add up to their financial woes
  • Vast majority of farmers lack the financial ability to own equipment such as tractors, seed planters, water motors, etc.
  • This deprives them of the ability to perform their tasks efficiently and with maximum profitability.

    • Our Solutions

    1. Connecting the buyer with the farmer

    Our platform enables the farmer to connect directly with the big players and sell their produce directly to them. Every part of the conversation is taken safely and securely ensuring both get the best results

    2. Connecting buyers with transportation facilitators

    Our platform allows the buyer to connect with a transport facilitator who would accept the job postings done by the buyer according to his convenience.

    3. Connecting Farmers

    The platform would allow farmers to post their requirements for equipment with all the necessary information provided by them such as duration, time, and budgets.
